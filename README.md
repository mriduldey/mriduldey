I am a full-stack software engineer who enjoys turning complex requirements into systems that are understandable, maintainable, and practical to operate.

Over the past seven years, I have worked across frontend applications, backend APIs, reusable components, platform foundations, and engineering automation. My primary experience is with **JavaScript, TypeScript, Java, Angular, React, Next.js, Node.js, and Spring Boot**.
